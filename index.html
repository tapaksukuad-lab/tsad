About Me

TSAD is an independent music artist whose works reflect deep emotions, personal stories, and meaningful journeys of life. Through heartfelt lyrics and melodies, TSAD shares experiences of love, distance, longing, and resilience, allowing listeners to connect with every song on a personal level. Known for touching tracks such as Kisah Dibalik Jarak, Selamanya Disisi, and Jarak Tak Memisahkan Hati, TSAD continues to craft music that speaks to the soul.

With a unique blend of emotional storytelling and relatable themes, TSAD’s music has become a companion for those navigating moments of joy, solitude, and healing. Every song is not only an expression of feeling but also a bridge that connects hearts, no matter the distance.
