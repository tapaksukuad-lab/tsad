About Me

Hi, I’m TSAD, an independent music artist who writes and performs songs inspired by real emotions, stories, and journeys of life.

My works blend heartfelt lyrics and melodies with themes of love, distance, and resilience, creating music that listeners can relate to on a personal level. Tracks like Kisah Dibalik Jarak and Selamanya Disisi have become companions for many during moments of solitude and reflection.

My Vision

Music is not just sound — it’s a bridge that connects hearts, no matter the distance. My vision is to keep crafting songs that inspire, heal, and bring people closer together.

Milestones

  • 2026 – Preparing upcoming albums
  • Growing global listeners via Spotify, YouTube Music, and TikTok
